Donate

Support your community today by making a tax deductible donation to Phoenix Pride and the Phoenix Pride Community Foundation!

The Phoenix Pride Community Foundation funds the Phoenix Pride Scholarship Program, Phoenix Pride Community Grants Program and the Phoenix Pride Partnership Program. Your contribution will help Phoenix Pride continue its mission to celebrate and promote the history, diversity and future prosperity of the metropolitan Phoenix LGBTQ community. Together with our donors, Phoenix Pride awards approximately $100K in grants and scholarships almost every year. This money goes back into the metropolitan Phoenix LGBTQ community strengthening organizations and assisting individuals in order to further the cause of equality and create a more prosperous community for all.

Donate today to support scholarships for a LGBTQ students, or to support a nonprofit organization seeking grant contributions to continue their services to the Phoenix LGBTQ community. You can choose which Phoenix Pride Program you would like to support. We also encourage you to dedicate your donation to a friend, family member or loved one. What better way to honor the memory of someone close to our hearts then by contributing to a charity organization that supports the future prosperity of the Phoenix LGBTQ community? Let us know if you wish to share your dedication with the community and we will post it on our site today.

To ensure the security of your donation though our website, it is managed by our nonprofit partner Network for Good’s Donor Advised Fund. To ensure the security of your donation through your mobile devise, it is managed by Give By Cell and the Mobile Giving Foundation.
